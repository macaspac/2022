I’m Leanne, a Toronto-based independent designer specializing in design, branding, and technology. Having spent almost 20 years building for the internet and designing software, I'm fascinated by our relationship to technology, its ethics, and how to empower communities with it. Currently, I'm exploring ways to express art in technology through creative coding libraries and 3D interactive illustrations, as well as building bespoke experiences for brands that want to make the internet fun again. I also lead operations at Recess↗, a community focused on exploring creative curiosity through workshops, events, installations, film, photography, and design.

While I enjoy my alone time tinkering away, I love helping others bring their ideas to life. I’m always open to collaborating with brands and startups that are looking for fun and functional ways to build and connect with their community. If any of this resonates with you, feel free to reach out via email at leanne@macaspac.ca.
