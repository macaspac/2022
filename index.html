I’m Leanne, a Toronto-based independent designer specializing in branding, design and technology. Currently, I’m exploring ways to express art in technology, from creative coding libraries to 3D illustration, and finding my nook in the small web. I also spend time with Recess↗, where we focus on instilling creative confidence through workshops, events, installations, film, photography, and design.

As much as I love my alone time tinkering away, I love helping others bring their ideas to life. I’m always open to collaborating with brands and startups that are looking for fun and functional ways to build and connect with their community. If any of this resonates with you, feel free to reach out via email at leanne@macaspac.ca.
