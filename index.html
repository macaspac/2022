I’m Leanne, a Toronto-based independent designer specializing in branding, design and technology. Currently, I’m tending to my 🌻digital garden↗, where I explore new avenues to create and play, including 3D illustration (blender and Cinema 4D), creative coding (matter.js, p5.js, TouchDesigner), and finding my nook in the small web. I also spend time with Recess↗, where we focus on instilling creative confidence for people who are looking to explore their creativity through workshops, events, installations, film, photography, and design.

As much as I love my alone time building new things, I love helping others bring their ideas to life. I’m also open to collaborating with brands and startups that are looking for fun and functional ways to build and connect with their community. If any of this resonates with you, feel free to reach out via email at leanne@macaspac.ca.
